OUR STORY:

✧･ﾟ*✧･ﾟ:* Kira Kira, means glittering or shining, and the goal for our brand is to create a kira kira life for our customers through our kira kira supplies. Kira Kira Supplies was created to help people begin their own journals, as we provide notebooks, pens, washi tape, and stickers to decorate your spreads. We wanted to create a website that customers could go to and easily find what they will need, as it could be overwhelming looking at all the options out there. For further guidance, we designed our products into categorized themes/collections. ✧･ﾟ*✧･ﾟ:*
